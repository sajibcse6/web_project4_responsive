A winter morning is misty and cold. There is dense fog everywhere. Sometimes the fog is so dense that the sun rays cannot get through it. Everywhere looks hazy. Things at distance can hardly be seen. The sun seems to rise late. Children and old people suffer from the pinching cold of the morning. Dew deops fall at night. When the morning sun plought. Chindren and old people gather straw and make fire to warm themselves. The old people bask in the sun. Then people likes to eat Chira, Mur, Khai and various kinds of pithas. Winter morning gradually vanishes as the day advances. Then sun goes up. fog melts away and go to their respective duties.
